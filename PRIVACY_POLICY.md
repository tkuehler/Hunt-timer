# Privacy Policy for Hunt Clock

**Last Updated: June 19, 2026**

## Overview

Hunt Clock ("the Extension") is a Chrome extension that provides hunting season countdown timers and information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Local Storage Data
The Extension stores the following data locally on your device using Chrome's storage API:
- Your selected state and county preferences
- Custom hunting seasons you create
- Your display name (optional)
- User interface preferences

This data is stored **only on your device** and is never transmitted to external servers.

### Geolocation Data (Optional)
When you choose to use the "Auto-Detect Location" feature, the Extension requests your geographic coordinates to determine your Texas county. This data is:
- Used only to identify your county via the OpenStreetMap Nominatim API
- Never stored permanently
- Never shared with third parties beyond the single geocoding request
- Requested only when you explicitly click the "Auto-Detect My Location" button

## How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:
- Display relevant hunting season information for your location
- Remember your preferences between sessions
- Provide personalized countdown timers

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT**:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your data with third parties for marketing
- Track your browsing activity
- Collect analytics or usage data

The only external service used is OpenStreetMap's Nominatim API for reverse geocoding when you opt to auto-detect your location.

## Data Storage

All user data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage APIs. No data is stored on external servers.

## Your Rights

You can:
- Clear all stored data by removing the extension
- Deny location permissions at any time
- Manually select your location instead of using auto-detect

## Third-Party Services

### OpenStreetMap Nominatim API
When using location auto-detection, we send your coordinates to OpenStreetMap's free geocoding service. Their privacy policy can be found at: https://osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy

### Google Fonts
The Extension loads fonts from Google Fonts. Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Children's Privacy

This Extension does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date.
