# Chrome Web Store Listing Information

## Extension Name
Hunt Clock

## Short Description (132 characters max)
A beautiful new tab with hunting season countdowns. Auto-detect your Texas county for TPWD-accurate season dates.

## Detailed Description
Transform your new tab into a hunter's dashboard with beautiful wilderness backgrounds and countdown timers to your favorite hunting seasons.

**Features:**
- Replace your new tab with stunning nature photography
- Countdown timers for deer, turkey, dove, duck, and more
- Texas county-specific hunting seasons from TPWD regulations
- Auto-detect your location to get accurate local season dates
- Add custom seasons for any game animal
- Daily rotating backgrounds matched to the current season
- Clean, distraction-free interface
- Works offline after initial load

**Texas Hunters:**
Select your Texas county to get accurate hunting season dates based on TPWD (Texas Parks and Wildlife Department) regulations. Supports all 254 Texas counties with specific dates for:
- Whitetail Deer (General, Youth, Archery)
- Spring and Fall Turkey
- Dove (North, Central, South Zones)
- Quail
- Duck and Goose
- Javelina
- Feral Hog
- And more!

**Privacy Focused:**
- All data stored locally on your device
- No accounts required
- No tracking or analytics
- Location only used when you request it

Perfect for hunters who want a quick glance at upcoming seasons every time they open a new tab.

## Permissions Justification

### Storage Permission
**Why needed:** To save user preferences (selected state/county, custom seasons, display name) locally so they persist between browser sessions.

### Geolocation Permission (if included)
**Why needed:** Optional feature that allows users to auto-detect their Texas county for accurate local hunting season dates. Only triggered when user clicks "Auto-Detect My Location" button. Data is only used for a single reverse geocoding request and is never stored.
